The challenge

Off-the-shelf tools rarely fit

Generic SaaS forces your team to bend their process to someone else's product, and you pay for seats and features you never use. Legacy systems quietly accumulate technical debt until every change is risky and slow. And the prototype that won an early demo often can't carry real users. Custom software solves the problem you actually have — and grows with you instead of holding you back.

FAQ

Custom software questions

How much does custom software cost?

Cost depends on scope — the number of features, integrations, and platforms involved. Custom software is priced by the work it takes, not a per-seat licence, so we start with a short discovery to define a clear scope and give you a transparent estimate before any build begins. Smaller, focused projects cost far less than full platforms.

How long does it take to build?

We work in short iterations and aim to put a usable version in front of you early, rather than disappearing for months. Timelines scale with scope: a focused tool or MVP is faster than a multi-module platform. We agree on milestones up front so you always know what is shipping and when.

Do we own the source code and IP?

Yes. You own 100% of the source code, intellectual property, and infrastructure we build for you. Everything lives in your repositories and your cloud accounts, and we hand over full access. There is no lock-in to a proprietary platform you can never leave.

Can you maintain it after launch?

Yes. We can stay on for ongoing maintenance, new features, and support, or hand everything over to your in-house team — your choice. Because we ship tested, documented code with CI/CD in place, the software is straightforward to maintain whether we continue or you take it forward.
