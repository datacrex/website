FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before reaching out.

How do we get started?

It starts with a free, no-obligation consultation. We learn your goals, scope the work, and send a clear plan and estimate — usually within one business day.

What does a project cost?

It depends on scope. We offer fixed-price projects when requirements are clear, and predictable monthly rates for dedicated teams. You'll always have an estimate before any work begins.

Who will actually work on my project?

Senior engineers and data scientists — the same people you meet on the first call. No layered subcontracting or hand-offs to junior teams.

Do I own the code and IP?

Yes — 100%. All code, models, and infrastructure we build are yours, with no vendor lock-in.

Can you work with our existing team and tools?

Absolutely. We can embed into your workflow and integrate with your stack, augment your team, or build end-to-end — whichever fits best.

How do you keep us informed?

Weekly demos, live progress, and clear milestones. You'll always know exactly where things stand — no black boxes.
